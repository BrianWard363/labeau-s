History

1981

Renee LaBeau and her husband Robert has always wanted to own a business so one day in early 1981 they saw a add for a drive-in up for sale in Garden City, Utah. Shortly after the place was boutght they changes the name from Western Drive-In to the well known name that is known as La Beau's. At first the business was slow, but as time went on the place continued to grow to be the most popular place at Bear lake.

1982

A year after LaBeau's opened a family loss almost made Renee to shut the doors of the restaurant as her husband Robert had passed.
