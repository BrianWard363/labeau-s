Reviews

Hannah Morgan

This place is so good! My family eats here every time we go to Bear lake and their food is always so yummy. The shakes are top notch! We always get the fresh raspberry shakes and they are delicious! The ice cream is so creamy and the raspberries are so sweet. There is always a long line but they are really fast at getting everyone's food out. I do not believe they have indoor dining but they have lots of picnic tables in a nice shady area.

Luke Rasmussen

The food is absolutely delicious. I personally love the pastrami burger. It's got a nice flavor, and it was as perfect as a burger could be. The fries are just the right balance of crispy and soft. And then, to top it all off, you have to get the famous raspberry shake. It's amazingly creamy and are definitely the best raspberry shakes around. Would definitely recommend coming here

Pamela H

I read about this place on a traveler blog and decided to go since they had raspberry shakes. Was not disappointed.
Got there early (11:30a) for lunch and the line was not very long. You order at the front outside window. Ordered the shake and a cheeseburger and fries. My shake came out first. It was thick, had some fresh raspberries, and was made with real ingredients. My burger and fries came out hot and I sat at an outside table and ate. If I lived there I would frequent this place when I was craving a real shake and a burger. 👍🏻

Jim Auto

Great food! The burger was juicy and tasty. The tots were crispy and yummy. The shake was out of this world delicious. Would come again. It's more on the expensive side, so make sure your wallet is ready.

Sean Bourne

Took our kids and their friends there for Bear Lake's famous raspberry shakes. There was only one other place open besides Labeau's, so it was quite busy. However, they got our order out fast. I was actually very surprised at how fast our order came up. The kids loved the shakes and always have a good time. Its a summer tradition.
Kid-friendliness: Very kid and family friendly environment.
Parking: It can get busy enough to the point parking is difficult to find, especially when all the establishments along the street are open and its the middle of summertime. There is additional parking across the street from Labeau's.

Elisa Black

You cant go to Bear Lake and not get a LaBeau's shake! We go every summer. The multiple flavors are unique and absolutely delicious. Theyve got your classic flavors, and also things like peanut butter, burnt almond fudge, and more. They are the best shakes I know of!
